About Us

Welcome to Apple Chase: Worm Odyssey, an exciting game inspired by the classic "Snake Xenzia" from Nokia 3310. Our game takes the nostalgic snake concept and adds a fresh twist, putting you in control of an apple facing various obstacles and stages.

Project Overview

Discover the world of Apple Chase: Worm Odyssey, a game that brings the classic Snake concept to life in a new way. Inspired by the iconic Nokia game, our goal is to revive the nostalgia while introducing innovative gameplay mechanics. Join us on this journey through time and technology!

Development Process

The project has been meticulously planned and executed in defined phases:

  • Conceptualization Phase (November 16, 2023, to January 5, 2024): Defines game mechanics and rules, with planning initiated from November 17 to November 19, 2023.
  • Game Design Phase (November 19 to November 22, 2023): Sets the creative direction of the game.
  • Programming Phase (November 19 to November 29, 2023): Implements core functionalities.
  • Testing and Debugging (November 29 to December 5, 2023): Rigorously evaluates the game's functionality.
  • User Interface Development (December 5 to December 25, 2023): Crafts the visual and interactive aspects of the game.
  • Polishing Phase (December 25 to December 30, 2023): Dedicated to refining graphics and animations.
  • Quality Assurance (December 30, 2023, to January 5, 2024): Ensures a stable and enjoyable gaming experience.
  • Documentation Phase (Concludes on January 5, 2024): Captures the technical and creative aspects of the development process.
  • Release Phase (January 5, 2024): Marks the culmination of the project, showcasing collaborative efforts and creativity.
